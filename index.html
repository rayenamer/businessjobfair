What is Bardo2Business

B2B 2024 is an event that brings together Tunisia's private sector, industry partners, support organizations, and public entities to foster innovation and collaboration. It offers exhibitions, hackathons, job fairs, and networking opportunities to stimulate business growth and knowledge sharing.
